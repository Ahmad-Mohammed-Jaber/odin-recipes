Falafel Sandwhich

A falfel sandwhich is one of the things that Jordanians like to have for breakfast

Ingrediants

  • Falafel
  • Hummus
  • Bread
  • Any number of additions (Tahini, pickles, etc)

Steps To Prepare

  1. Cook the falafel
  2. Open Bread
  3. Spread the hummus in bread
  4. Put falafel in and addtions
Enjoy!

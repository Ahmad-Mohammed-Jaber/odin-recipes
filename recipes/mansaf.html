Mansaf

Mansaf is Jordan's most popular dish, it is what I look forward to eat when I come to Jordan!

Ingrediants

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Almonds
  • Jameed (Jordanian Thing)
  • Almonds
  • Bread

Steps To Prepare

  1. Cut the Almonds if already not cut into halfs
  2. Boil the almonds in a pan with oil
  3. Cook the rice and the meat
  4. Cover serving pan with bread
  5. Put rice and meat on top of it
  6. Pour some Jameed on it
  7. Sprinke some almonds
Enjoy!
